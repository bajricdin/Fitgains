FITGAINS FAQs

What is Fitgains?

Fitgains is a fitness platform designed to help users create personalized workout plans based on their specific fitness goals, body type, and available equipment. Whether you're looking to build muscle, lose weight, or improve your overall fitness, Fitgains tailors workouts to your needs.

How do I create a workout plan?

Simply fill out the form on our "Workout Plan" page with your age, fitness level, goals, and equipment availability. Our system will generate a customized workout plan for you, including exercises, sets, reps, and rest times.

Can Fitgains help me with nutrition advice?

Fitgains focuses primarily on fitness and workout plans. While we do not provide detailed nutrition plans, we offer general guidance on maintaining a balanced diet for your fitness goals. We recommend consulting a nutritionist for a more personalized meal plan.

Do I need an account to use Fitgains?

No, you don't need an account to use Fitgains. Our platform allows you to generate workout plans without any registration or login. However, we may offer the option to save your workout plans in the future for easier access.

Can I adjust my workout plan later?

Absolutely! If you feel that a workout is too challenging or too easy, you can return to the "Workout Plan" page and make adjustments to your preferences. Fitgains will generate a new plan based on your updated input.

Is Fitgains suitable for beginners?

Yes! Fitgains caters to all fitness levels, from beginners to advanced athletes. When creating your workout plan, simply select your fitness level, and we will customize the plan to suit your experience and abilities.

Are there any costs associated with using Fitgains?

Fitgains is currently free to use! Our goal is to provide accessible fitness solutions to anyone looking to improve their health and fitness. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the basic workout plan generator will always be free.

Can I track my progress on Fitgains?

While Fitgains currently focuses on generating workout plans, we encourage you to track your progress separately. You can record your workouts and monitor your improvements using fitness apps or journals.
